Call for Applications: United Front Volunteer Programme 2025

  • Admin

Are you a young changemaker with a passion for community development, climate change action, and social justice? United Front for Development and Climate Action is looking for committed volunteers to join its team in their mission to accelerate sustainable peace and development across Nigeria. This opportunity is open for interested and qualified candidates who wish to gain hands-on experience, develop leadership skills, and work with a vibrant team of young changemakers making a real impact.

Application Now Closed

(Deadline 28th February, 2025)

About United Front Africa

Founded in 2022, United Front Africa is a youth-led non-profit organization working to accelerate sustainable peace and development in Northeast Nigeria through strategic partnerships, volunteerism, technology, and community-driven innovations. We seek to build a frontier of action to address the root causes of human insecurities, safeguard the rights of poor and vulnerable groups, improve livelihoods, and strengthen communities' resilience to climate change. Our work centers on developing and scaling innovative solutions, building new-generation changemakers, and working with key stakeholders to address the vicious circles of poverty, insecurity, corruption, and climate change.

Volunteer Categories:


Community Development: Support initiatives that aim to improve livelihoods, promote education, capacity development, and enhance community resilience.

Climate Action: Help raise awareness of climate change, encourage sustainable environmental practices, and promote community-led solutions to environmental challenges.

Social Justice:Advocate for social equity, inclusive governance, and the protection of vulnerable communities, with a focus on human rights and environmental justice.

What You Will Gain:

Thought this is non-paying position, United Front Africa is committed to providing volunteers with a wide range of personal and professional benefits:
1. Training and Capacity Building: Access robust training sessions that cover community development, climate action, and social justice advocacy.
2. Leadership Development: Gain practical leadership experience by participating in projects and campaigns that impact your community.
3. Networking Opportunities: Build connections with professionals, activists, and like-minded youth across Northeast Nigeria and beyond.
4. Nonprofit Experience: Deepen your understanding of the nonprofit sector and develop skills in areas such as project management, advocacy, and community organizing.
5. Certificates and Recognition: Volunteers will receive certificates of participation, letters of recommendation, and opportunities to attend regional and national events.

Eligibility Criteria:

• Age: 15 - 25 years old
• Location: Must be from one of the six states in Northeast Nigeria (Borno, Adamawa, Yobe, Gombe, Bauchi, Taraba)
• Commitment: A minimum of 6 hours per week for a period of 3 to 6 months
• Passion: Strong interest in community development, climate action, or social justice
• Skills: Effective communication, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities
• Education: Minimum of a National Diploma; young people at the early stage of their careers in the nonprofit sector are encouraged to apply

How to Apply:

You will be required to provide the following:
1. A Statement of Interest (300 words max) explaining your motivation to volunteer with United Front Africa and how you plan to contribute to your senatorial district.
2. A CV/Resume highlighting your education, experience, and skills.
3. Contact Information (phone number and email) among others.
